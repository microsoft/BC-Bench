Title: [master] Wrong GST calculation in Sales/Purchase Invoice when Allocation Account is selected and Prices Incl. VAT is set
Repro Steps:
Actions: Create Allocation Account TEST, add 2 lines with "Inherit from parent" account type, set Share 40 and 60. Assign dimensions for each line - Department SALES for one, Department PROD for another line. Create Sales/Purchase Invoice, set Prices Incl. VAT = true, add line with G/L Account type, set account no. (for example 8110 Cleaning), Quantity 1, Unit Price Incl. VAT 1250, Allocation Account No. TEST. Press Preview Posting or post the invoice. Check G/L Entries. Expected result: 5 G/L Entries were created: Two entries for G/L Account 8110 Cleaning with Amount (LCY) -400 and -600. Two entries for G/L Account 5610 Sales VAT 25% with Amount (LCY) -100 and -150. One entry for G/L Account 2310 Customers Domestic with Amount (LCY) 1250. Actual result: 5 G/L Entries were created: Two entries for G/L Account 8110 Cleaning with Amount (LCY) -320 and -480. Two entries for G/L Account 5610 Sales VAT 25% with Amount (LCY) -80 and -120. One entry for G/L Account 2310 Customers Domestic with Amount (LCY) 1000. If you remove Allocation Account No. and preview posting again, the amounts will be correct: If you clear flag Prices Incl. VAT, the amount will be also correct.
