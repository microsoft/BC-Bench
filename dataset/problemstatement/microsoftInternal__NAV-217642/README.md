Title: [master] [ALL-E] Unit Cost is Not Visible in Price List Lines because ResourceAsset variable is not initialized
Repro Steps:
Successfully reproduced in CRONUS GB v26.0 BC26 Ensure 'New Sales Pricing Experience' is turned on in Feature Management Create a new resource and navigate “Purchase Prices”: On page “Price List Line Review (7005, List)”, in case of resource, the field “Unit Cost” isn’t visible: Cx's opinion: In procedure “UpdateColumnVisibility” of page "Price List Line Review", the variable “ResourceAsset” isn’t initialized. More Information from partner:Regarding the Unit Cost field on the price list lines: in the previous version, this field was indeed available for Resources. During the upgrade process, the values were correctly migrated for those entries, so the field should be available and functional, as expected. Additionally, I would like to clarify that the Unit Cost field is not marked with Visible = False in the page definition. Instead, its visibility is controlled by a variable, which unfortunately appears to be incorrectly initialized — as I already pointed out in my previous emails. This is the core of the issue and should be considered a bug rather than an intentional design choice.Using the Designer is not a viable solution for our customers in this case, especially since the visibility is driven by a variable. Relying on such a workaround would not only create inconsistencies across pages in Business Central, but it would also undermine the logic of conditional visibility in other standard areas of the application.We therefore believe this should be addressed as a defect in the initialization logic of the visibility condition, and not treated as expected behavior.
