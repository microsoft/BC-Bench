Title: [ALL-E] When a contact is created from the vendor card, it does not pull through the vendor company or type onto contact card fields.
Repro Steps:
Refer attached word document for Detailed repro. When a contact is created from the vendor card. It does not pull through the vendor company or type onto contact card fields. There are two ways in which the customer creates a conztact. Scenario 1: Search for Vendors. On the ribbon, Choose Vendor ->Related > vendor > other > contact > this will take you to the contact list > select new. You will see the contact card will already be populated with type person, and company No. Scenario 2: Go to the vendor card. Click New to create a New Vendor. Select Vendor Company Template. Go to the address & contact fast tab. On the 'primary contact code' field there should be three dots. click the three dots. Then select '‘New'. You will now see the contact card has been created. but the vendor company has not pulled through. it’s essentially a blank card. Can you see why this is occurring? as i would guess if you made a contact this way from the vendor card, company name and type person should also pull through like i explained in the first method. Yet in the second method this does not occur, The customer is on Version 23.5 SAAS i have tried this in my own cronus and i get the same result.
